About us


We are a family of Mexicans proud of the crafts of their country.

Jásï was created in 2021 by the GP family, with the purpose of helping its customers find unique and original mexican pieces of garments, accesories, jewerly and decor.

We work with a group of artisans throughout the Mexican Republic with whom we make the different pieces. Each of these pieces are unique for the most part, as each craftsman plays with his own techniques and imagination.

This alliance has allowed us to help many families to have a secure income through the fair sale for their admirable work.
